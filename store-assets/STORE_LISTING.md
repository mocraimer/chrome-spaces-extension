## Detailed Description (for the listing page)
🚀 Chrome Spaces — Workspace Manager for Chrome

Turn tab chaos into organized productivity. Chrome Spaces transforms every browser window into a named workspace ("space"), making it effortless to manage multiple projects, research sessions, or work contexts.

✨ KEY FEATURES

• Named Workspaces — Give each Chrome window a meaningful name (e.g., "Project Alpha", "Research", "Personal")
• Instant Switching — Jump between spaces with keyboard shortcuts (Ctrl+Shift+Left/Right)
• Restore Closed Spaces — Accidentally closed a window? Restore it with all its tabs intact
• Move Tabs Between Spaces — Easily relocate tabs to the right workspace
• Fuzzy Search — Find any space instantly by typing, with keyboard navigation
• Smart Matching — Chrome Spaces reconnects your windows after browser restarts
• Import/Export — Back up and share your workspace configurations
• Grid Arrange — Tile all your windows across your screen with one click

⌨️ KEYBOARD-FIRST DESIGN

• Alt+Shift+A — Open the Spaces popup
• Ctrl+Shift+Left/Right — Navigate between spaces
• Arrow keys — Browse through spaces in the popup
• / — Focus the search bar
• Enter — Switch to the selected space

🔒 PRIVACY-FIRST

Chrome Spaces stores ALL data locally on your device. No accounts, no cloud, no tracking, no analytics. Your browsing data never leaves your browser.

💡 PERFECT FOR

• Developers juggling multiple projects
• Researchers managing topic-specific tab collections
• Students organizing coursework by subject
• Anyone who wants their browser to feel as organized as their desktop

Built with TypeScript and React. Open source at https://github.com/mocraimer/chrome-spaces-extension
